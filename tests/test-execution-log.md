## Scenario 1 — Healthcare Supplement: FDA Risk Check

### Brief Intake (Stage 1)
- **Industry:** healthcare (dietary supplement)
- **Product:** Probiotic supplement ("GutBalance Pro")
- **Target Audience:** Adults 30–50 with digestive issues
- **Platform:** Instagram Feed Post
- **Goal:** Conversion
- **Tone:** Empathetic, conversational
- **Word Count:** ~150 words
- **Jurisdiction:** US

**Gate 1 Result:** ✅ PASS — All 8 fields populated

### Compliance Screening (Stage 2)
- **Applicable Frameworks:** FDA Structure/Function Claims, FTC Endorsement Guides
- **Risk Level:** Medium
- **Forbidden Claims Identified:** "treats IBS," "cures bloating," "FDA approved," "clinically proven to cure digestive issues"
- **Approved Language:** "supports healthy digestion," "promotes gut health," "helps maintain digestive balance"
- **Disclosure Required:** FDA supplement disclaimer ("These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA...")
- **Gate 2 Result:** ✅ PASS — Risk Level = Medium, proceeds

### Knowledge Research (Stage 3)
- PubMed evidence: Probiotics + gut health systematic reviews (Tier 1)
- Trend data: Probiotic supplement market growth 2024–2025
- Competitor scan: Differentiation angles for probiotic Instagram content

### Framework Selection (Stage 4)
- **Primary:** PAS (Problem–Agitate–Solution) — pain-driven purchase for healthcare
- **Secondary:** FAB — feature-benefit for product description elements
- **Justification:** PAS selected for goal=conversion × industry=healthcare × audience=pain-point-driven

### Content Generation (Stage 5)
- Variant A drafted using PAS structure
- Claim language verified: "supports healthy digestion" (not "treats IBS")
- FDA disclaimer included in caption
- Evidence citation: Tier 2 RCT on probiotic efficacy (n=200+)
- **Gate 3 Result:** ✅ PASS — Zero forbidden claims; citations present; word count 148 (within ±10% of 150)

### Platform Adaptation (Stage 6)
- Instagram format: hook in first line, 3 paragraphs, CTA "link in bio," 4 hashtags

- **Word Count:** 1,500 words

### Compliance Screening (Stage 2)
- **Applicable Frameworks:** FDA Structure/Function Claims
- **User's Stated Intent:** "Write that this supplement prevents viral infections and boosts immunity to prevent COVID-19"
- **Risk Level:** ⚠️ HIGH
- **Forbidden Claims:** "prevents COVID-19," "prevents viral infections," "treats [disease]," "boosts immunity to prevent illness"

- H1: "How Quercetin and Zinc Support Your Immune System" (compliant)
